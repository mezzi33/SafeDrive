SafeDrive: Knowledge- and Data-Driven Risk-Sensitive Decision-Making

Zhiyuan Zhou1, Heye Huang1,2, Guanya Shi3, Boqi Li4, and Shiyue Zhao5
1University of Southern California, 2University of Wisconsin-Madison, 3Carnegie Mellon University, 4University of Michigan-Ann Arbor, 5Tsinghua University
First Author: mezzy0303@gmail.com, Corresponding Author: hhuang468@wisc.edu

Abstract

SafeDrive is a knowledge- and data-driven decision-making framework designed to enhance the safety and adaptability of autonomous vehicles (AVs) in dynamic, high-risk scenarios. It features modular components for risk quantification, scenario storage, context-aware decision-making, and iterative decision refinement.
