Abstract

Recent advancements in autonomous vehicles (AVs) leverage Large Language Models (LLMs) to perform well in normal driving scenarios. However, ensuring safety in dynamic, high-risk environments and managing safety-critical long-tail events remains a significant challenge. The SafeDrive framework enhances AV safety and adaptability through modular design, extensive evaluation, and real-world validation.
